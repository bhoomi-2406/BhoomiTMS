Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

You can register for any event by navigating to the "Events" page from our main site. Click on the event you're interested in, and you'll find a registration form with all the necessary details and instructions.

How can I become a volunteer?

We are always looking for enthusiastic volunteers! Please submit a query through the form above, selecting "Volunteer Opportunities." Our team will get in touch with you with more information on how to join us.

How can my company sponsor an event?

For all sponsorship inquiries, please use the contact form and select "Sponsorship & Partnership Inquiry." Our events team will review your request and reach out to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

I'm experiencing a technical issue with the website.

We're sorry to hear that! Please provide a detailed description of the issue you are facing in the query form, selecting "Website Technical Issues." Our support team will work to resolve it as quickly as possible.

Media and Press Inquiries

For all media and press-related questions, please use the contact form above, selecting the "Media and Press Inquiries" option. Our media relations team will respond to your request promptly.

Lost and Found during an Event

If you have lost or found an item at one of our events, please contact a staff member at the event's information desk. Alternatively, you can use the contact form above, choosing the "Lost and Found" query type, and we will assist you as soon as possible.

General Information

For any other questions not covered in the FAQs, please submit a query using the form above with the "General Information" option. We're here to help!
